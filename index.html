About Genius Developers Uganda

Genius Developers Uganda is a website and software development agency under Elijah Kitondekiggya aka Genius Official who is also the CEO of the Genius Empire and the headquarters are found in Kampala-Banda and in Entebbe as well

Founded in the heart of Kampala, Genius Developers Uganda has grown into a premier digital solutions provider serving clients across Africa and globally. We combine local market understanding with world-class technical expertise to deliver solutions that truly resonate with African audiences while meeting international standards.

Our team of passionate developers, designers, and strategists believes technology should solve real problems and create meaningful impact. We've proudly served over 200 clients including startups, enterprises, NGOs, churches, and government agencies since our inception.
